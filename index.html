About us

Beautysalon is a beauty salon established for more than 35 years in the market and with a team of more than 30 professional consultants qualified in national and international courses.


Investing well to meet the physical structure from the beginning, the Beautysalon has become a modern space designed to meet the most demanding customers.


All clients undergo a hair diagnosis to give start of treatments adjusted to each hair type always the nature of each one. Going through reeducation capillary, in which new ways of taking care of the hair, from choosing products for your profile, until its correct application, which will perpetuate the effect of home treatments.
